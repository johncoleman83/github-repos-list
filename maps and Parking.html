Parking
Ceremony Parking at Alice Millar Chapel

Please allow at least 15 minutes to find parking and to walk to the chapel. On Saturdays and Sundays, there is FREE parking in Northwestern Parking Lots in various locations around the Northwestern Campus and NU Alice Millar Chapel. The lots are labeled faculty or permit parking, but that does not apply on Saturdays as long as it is a Northwestern Parking lot. Some street parking is free East of the Chapel on Saturdays, but some street parking is Metered Parking in other areas on Saturdays. On Sundays, there is always FREE Street Parking in all of Evanston.

Reception Parking

For guests not staying overnight at the Hilton Garden Inn, there is a parking Garage next to the hotel that will cost $8.00- for parking for the evening. This is not necessary for guests of the Hilton Garden Inn.

Alice Millar Chapel Hotel Parking

Parking: Overnight Parking is for $13- and covers unlimited access to the parking lot for 24 hours.
