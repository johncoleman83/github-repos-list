Mustard's Last Stand

Mustard's Last Stand is where we went to celebrate our engagement right after John "popped the question". It's a staple of Evanston; burgers, dogs, and sausages joint located adjacent to Ryan Field, serving many Wildcats and non-Wildcats alike. Recommended if you're looking for an authentic Chicago 'dog!
